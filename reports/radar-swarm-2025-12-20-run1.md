# Executive Brief for Tubi Leadership

## TLDR
Netflix's acquisition of Warner Bros. and HBO Max poses a significant threat, reshaping the competitive landscape. However, Tubi has opportunities to capitalize on its fast-growing catalogue and the rise of ad-supported viewing to capture a larger share of the streaming ad revenue.

## Critical Threats

1. **Netflix's Acquisition of Warner Bros. and HBO Max (Severity: 10)**
   - **Action:** Develop a strategic response plan focusing on differentiating Tubi's unique value proposition. Consider partnerships or exclusive content deals to enhance Tubi's competitive edge.

2. **Amazon's Migration of Freevee Content to Prime Video (Severity: 8)**
   - **Action:** Accelerate efforts to capture Freevee's user base by enhancing Tubi's content offerings and marketing campaigns targeting free streaming users.

3. **The Roku Channel Surpassing Tubi in U.S. TV Market Share (Severity: 7)**
   - **Action:** Invest in targeted marketing and user acquisition strategies to reclaim market share. Explore partnerships to expand content offerings and enhance user engagement.

## Strategic Opportunities

1. **Capitalize on Tubi's Fast Catalogue Growth (Value: 9, Feasibility: 8)**
   - **Next Steps:** Launch a targeted advertising campaign highlighting Tubi's extensive and diverse content library to attract more advertisers and viewers.

2. **Capture a Larger Share of the Projected $17 Billion Streaming Ad Revenue (Value: 9, Feasibility: 7)**
   - **Next Steps:** Enhance ad technology and targeting capabilities to offer advertisers more value. Develop partnerships with major brands to increase ad revenue streams.

3. **Leverage Amazon's Freevee Shutdown (Value: 8, Feasibility: 7)**
   - **Next Steps:** Implement a user acquisition strategy focused on Freevee's displaced audience. Highlight Tubi's free streaming model in marketing efforts to attract these users.

## Market Trends

- **Consolidation in Streaming:** Monitor the impact of Netflix's acquisition of Warner Bros. and HBO Max on the competitive landscape.
- **Rise of Ad-Supported Viewing:** Ad-supported viewing is peaking, presenting significant opportunities for Tubi to expand its audience and ad revenue.
- **Shift to Premium Content:** YouTube's acquisition of global streaming rights to the Oscars signals a shift in premium content distribution, challenging traditional broadcasters.

## This Week's Priority

**Focus on capturing Freevee's displaced user base by enhancing Tubi's content offerings and launching targeted marketing campaigns.**
